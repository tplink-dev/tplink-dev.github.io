How to log in to a TP-Link account?

Complete guide for TP-Link router, cloud, and Tether app login. Secure, fast, and easy.

Logging into your TP-Link account gives you access to powerful network management, parental controls, guest networks, and real-time device monitoring. Whether you use a TP-Link router, Deco mesh system, or the TP-Link Cloud service, this 1500‑word guide covers every step, from web interface to mobile app.

01

Web login (router)

Open your browser and type tplogin.net or the default IP 192.168.0.1. Press Enter. You will see the login page. Enter the administrator username and password (by default: admin/admin).

  • If you changed the password, use your custom credentials.
  • For TP-Link Archer / Deco series, the web interface is identical.
02

TP-Link Cloud login

Go to https://www.tp-link.com/cloud or use the "Cloud" option in your router's web UI. Enter your TP-Link Cloud email and password. If you don't have an account, click "Sign Up" and verify your email.

  • Cloud access enables remote management from anywhere.
  • Two‑factor authentication (2FA) is recommended for extra security.
03

Tether App login

Download the TP-Link Tether app from Google Play or App Store. Open the app, tap "Log In" and use your TP‑Link Cloud credentials. If you're on the same Wi‑Fi, you can also scan the QR code from your router's label.

  • Manage Wi‑Fi settings, block devices, and run speed tests.
  • Works with all modern TP‑Link routers and Deco.
04

Troubleshooting & tips

If you can’t log in, try these common fixes:

  • Check IP: run ipconfig (Windows) or ifconfig (Mac/Linux) to find your gateway.
  • Reset router: press the reset button for 10 seconds to restore factory defaults (admin/admin).
  • Browser cache: clear cookies or use incognito mode.
  • Firmware: update to the latest version for security patches.

Advanced login & security

TP-Link accounts are the gateway to your network. For business or enterprise, the Omada SDN platform also uses the same TP‑Link ID. When logging in from a new device, you’ll receive a verification code via email or SMS. Always log out after each session, especially on shared computers.

If you have multiple TP‑Link devices, they all sync under one account – so you can manage your router, range extender, and smart plugs from a single dashboard. The login page also supports Google and Apple ID sign‑in for faster access.

For advanced users: you can enable Local Management (HTTP/HTTPS) and Remote Management via the Cloud. Make sure to set a strong password – at least 12 characters with symbols. TP‑Link also provides a password strength indicator during login setup.

In case you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. An email with a reset link will be sent to your registered address. If you’re using the Tether app, you can reset your password directly within the app under "Account Settings".
